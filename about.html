About Granth – The Book World

A cosy local bookstore built for readers, students and quiet explorers.

Our Story

Granth – The Book World started as a small neighbourhood shop, created with one simple idea: books should feel within reach for everyone. From school students to working professionals and lifelong readers, our shelves are arranged to make browsing easy and enjoyable.

We focus on hand-picked titles across fiction, mystery, sci-fi, romance, horror and non-fiction so that every visit gives you something new to discover.

Our Goal

Our goal is to build a space where people can slow down, read peacefully and talk about stories. We support local authors where possible and regularly add fresh titles based on what our readers ask for.

If you are looking for a particular book, you can always ask us in person when you visit the store – we are happy to help.

Important Message

We do not accept online orders.
Please kindly visit the shop in person for reading or buying products.

You can use the contact details on this website to ask about stock or availability, but purchases and reading are done only at the store.
